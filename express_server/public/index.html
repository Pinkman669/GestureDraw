Draw Something

Introduction

In this game, you will be drawing with your bare hand.
Your finger act as the pen and draw on the screen.
Get your camera and your hands ready.
Let's have some fun!

How to play

Drawing by doing a OK gesture.
Handed point up to clear.
Make a fist closed to start over!
