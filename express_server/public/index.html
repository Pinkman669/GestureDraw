Draw Something

Introduction

In this game, you will be drawing with your bare hand. Your finger act as the pen and draw on the screen. Get your camera and your hands ready. Let's have some fun.

How to play

Use your index finger as the pen, and draw the image that shows up on the screen within the time limit. There are three mode in this game, training, count down and free draw mode. You can practice in the training mode, then move on to the countdown mode. You can also put in your own creativity in our free draw mode by drawing your own image.
